## word-of-the-day
> a daily game where you roll a random word from the english dictionary and see how rare it is! build your collection of trading cards by signing up for an account

built using svelte, supabase, and vercel (slop slop sorry i dont have money for backend)

you can roll a word once a day, if you sign up for an account! words do not save if you do not have an account, but you can roll as much as you'd like. share your word by clicking the share button! it copies the image of your trading card to your clipboard. 

live at https://wotd.miniaturity.com
